About Us

Vem Juice is an e-liquid brewery established in Surabaya on 2022. We have a vision to bring you the best e-liquid the market can offer with unique and new flavors that makes us different than other brewers.

Products

Creamsie Whimsie is an e-liquid we established using our own imaginary food. It is described as a deep fried ball of banana then we cover it with melted chocolate and coat with chopped peanuts. This e-liquid will surely give you new flavors to your vape experience.

Cool Water is an e-liquid we made with the inspirations of fresh water. It will give you the sensation of a cool water with a few hints of citrus that will make u coming back for more.

Grooven by RRQ Xinn is an e-liquid we made in collaboration with the e-sport player named xinn. this e-liquid has a base flavor of strawberry cake. You will get the sensation of a creamy strawberry cake with the freshness of the strawberry.
